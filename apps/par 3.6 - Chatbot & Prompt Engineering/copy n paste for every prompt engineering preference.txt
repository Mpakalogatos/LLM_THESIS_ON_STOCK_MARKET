Chain of Thought Prompting

You are a financial analyst.

Question: Should an investor consider buying stock in NVIDIA (NVDA) at this time, based on recent performance trends?

Let's think step by step.
1. Identify what NVIDIA does and its main business areas.
2. Consider the recent market and sector performance.
3. Evaluate any recent company trends (e.g., AI demand, GPU sales, earnings growth).
4. Consider potential risks or volatility.
5. Then, summarize with a concise investment view.

============================================================================================================ 

Self-Consistency Prompting

Should an investor consider buying Tesla (TSLA) stock at this time, given recent company performance, market trends, and industry risks?

Generate 3 independent reasoning paths. Each path should:
1. Analyze Tesla's business fundamentals.
2. Consider recent market trends and news.
3. Discuss potential risks and opportunities.
4. Conclude with a short investment recommendation.

ReAct (Reason + Act) example

You are a financial assistant that can reason and take actions. 

Question:
Check the recent stock price trend for Apple (AAPL) and provide a brief summary.

Use the following format:
- Reason: Think through how to analyze the stock.
- Action: Specify what you would do (e.g., fetch data from an API).
- Observation: Report the result of the action.
- Reasoning: Analyze the observation.
- Answer: Give the final summary.

Prompt Chaining example

Step 1 – Financial Summary:
You are a financial analyst.
Task: Summarize Tesla's (TSLA) most recent quarterly earnings and overall financial performance in 3-4 concise sentences. Output only the summary.

Step 2 – Market Trend Analysis:

You are a financial analyst.
Task: Analyze Tesla's market trends based on the financial summary. Consider EV sector growth, competitor activity, and potential risks. Output a concise market trend analysis.
Financial Summary:
"Tesla reported strong quarterly earnings with revenue of $25.3B, up 20% YoY. Profit margins improved due to higher vehicle deliveries and energy product growth. Cash flow remains strong, supporting continued expansion. Overall, Tesla’s financial fundamentals are solid."

Step 3 – Investment Recommendation:

You are a financial analyst.
Task: Based on the financial summary and market trend analysis, provide a short investment recommendation for Tesla stock. Include a brief rationale.
Financial Summary:
"Tesla reported strong quarterly earnings with revenue of $25.3B, up 20% YoY. Profit margins improved due to higher vehicle deliveries and energy product growth. Cash flow remains strong, supporting continued expansion. Overall, Tesla’s financial fundamentals are solid."
Market Trend Analysis: "Tesla benefits from rapid EV sector growth and increasing global demand for electric vehicles. Competitors are expanding, but Tesla maintains a technology and brand advantage. Risks include regulatory changes, production bottlenecks, and high stock valuation."
